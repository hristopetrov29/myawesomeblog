Cauliflower Crust Pizza Bites

  • Time: 25-30 minutes
  • Serves: 14

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (180ºC).
Step 2: Grate the cauliflower into a large bowl.
Step 3: Sprinkle salt over cauliflower and mix together. Set aside for 20 minutes while salt draws moisture out of cauliflower.
Step 4: Pour the cauliflower out onto 2 paper towels. Wrap up the shredded cauliflower and wring it out to drain the excess moisture from the cauliflower.
Step 5: Transfer the cauliflower to a large mixing bowl. Combine the remaining ingredients with cauliflower and mix.
Step 6: On a prepared baking sheet, form little circular pizza crusts with mixture, about ¼ inch thick.
Step 7: Bake for 25- 30 minutes.
Step 8: Spoon marinara and mozzarella onto each crust, top the rest with whatever toppings you like.
Step 9: Bake for 5-7 additional minutes (until cheese is melted).
Step 10: Enjoy!

Ingredients Quantity Price
heads cauliflower 2 8$
parmesan cheese 1/4 cup or 25g 8$
eggs 3 2$

Fruit Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1

Step 1: Bring orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, orange zest, and lemon zest to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
Step 2: Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.
Step 3: Layer the fruit in a large, clear glass bowl in this order: pineapple, strawberries, kiwi fruit, bananas, oranges, grapes, and blueberries.
Step 4: Pour the cooled sauce over the fruit. Cover and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving.

Ingredients Quantity Price
fresh juice 3/4 cup 2$
packed brown sugar 1/3 cup 1$
bananas, sliced 3 1$
oranges, peeled and sectioned 2 1$
kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced 3 2.5$

Tarator

  • Time: 7 minutes
  • Serves: 5 cups

Step 1: Cut the cucumbers into cubes and put them in a bowl. You may aslo grate them but it changes the look and the consistency. Beat the yogurt with a fork until it gets liquid and pour it over the cucumbers. Add the crushed garlic, the walnuts and the minced dill as well as salt and oil to taste. If needed add some water to make the soup as liquid as you like but take care not to make it too "thin". Put into the refrigerator to cool or add ice cubes. Serve cold.
